    Spiritual Amours for Christian Soldier's. MEMORY VERSE: Finally my brethren, be strong in the Lord and in the power of His Might. Put on the whole armour of God so that you may be able to stand against the schemes of the Devil. Ephesians 6:10-11 The Apostle Paul wrote this epistle to strengthen and equip the Church and He told us how we can live victoriously in this present evil ages inspite of Satanic opposition. Beloved we are in war and being strong in the Lord and his power Is very for living as s victorious Christian . God is our source of power and our strength is maintained if we remain in fellowship with the Lord. D.L Moody once said " When a Man has no strength, if he leans on God he becomes powerful " Our Strength is find in the Lord and his the only source of strength. Blessed is the one whose Strength is in the Lord. What is your hope and strength in? Is it in your Parents, The States or the worldly treasures. All this is sinking Sands. Many in around us are growing weary and weak but those who hope in the Lord shall renew their strength Let us remain in the Lord because we cannot grow outside him. I encourage you to put on the whole armor of God to be able to stand against Satan's deceptions. I pray the grace of the Lord will strengthen you in your weakness. May the grace of the Lord be with you all.
