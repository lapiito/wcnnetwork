Vision & Mission

To become a global Network to Save lost Souls, Equip , Disciple and Empower lives around the globe . We are dedicated to train, equip and raising leaders to make a significant impact. We believe in transforming lives and advancing the Kingdom of God

Leadership

Our leaders consider it a privilege and blessing to be used by God to serve the global church to raise, equip and to send out leaders to continue with the great Commission. We believe in transforming lives through the Word of God.

Digital Ministry

Our Digital Ministry enables us to reach and worship with our participants across the world. The Internet is a powerful tool that affords us the opportunity to engage in prayer, preaching and empowerment programs with our members globally.

Other Pillars

Watchmen Charismatic Network consists of other sectors such as Prayer and Intercession, Evangelism and Missions and Foundation.
