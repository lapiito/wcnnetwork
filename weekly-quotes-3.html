    Pursuing The Lover of Your Heart Jeremiah 24:7 [7]And I will give them a heart to know me, that I am the LORD: and they shall be my people, and I will be their God: for they shall return unto me with their whole heart. Our problem is that Most of us don't really know Him and we have never really been Hungry to know him. We have allowed things of this realm to satisfy our lives and satiate our hunger. We come to God day after day, week after week, year after year, just to have Him fill in the little empty spaces. Beloved God is tired of being "second place" to everything else in our lives. Many Know God but don't walk with him. We sometimes get so caught up in doing things for Him that we forget about Him. You can get so caught up in being "religious" that you never become spiritual. Beloved let's develop intimacy with the Lord. The Lord is ready to draw closer to you and let you experience his presence like never before. Pray now 1.Father I thank you for your word this day 2. Father help me to seek you wholeheartedly 3.Purify,mend, fix and Fill me again Lord God 4.Oh God release me from dead religion into spiritual hunger 5.Father we want your presence. we are so hungry.
